Snowy Cafe Niseko is a cafe and hospitality venue in the Niseko area. We were commissioned to photograph the space for use across their social media, website and marketing materials.

The shoot covered the cafe interior, the food and beverage presentation, exterior architecture and the surrounding winter environment. Images were produced to communicate the warmth and character of the space to an international audience visiting or planning to visit Niseko.
