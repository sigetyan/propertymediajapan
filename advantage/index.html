Speed matters

On the market
by tomorrow

In real estate, the window between accepting a listing and going live matters. The longer a property sits unlisted, the more momentum is lost. We built our entire production process around one principle: agents should be able to go live the day after we shoot.

That means a same-day cull, same-night edit, and delivery by morning. It has been our standard since we started, and it remains the thing agents mention most when they refer us.

Day 1Shoot
We photograph the property

Typically one to two hours on site for a residential shoot. We handle lighting, angles and staging guidance.

Night 1Edit
Images are culled and edited

Every image is colour corrected, exposure balanced and retouched to a consistent standard before delivery.

Day 2Deliver
Files arrive in your inbox

High-resolution JPEGs and web-optimised versions, ready to upload directly to portals and print collateral.

Day 2Live
You go live

The listing is published while buyer interest from the initial announcement is still fresh. No waiting, no follow-up emails.

Local knowledge

We live and
work here

Being based in Niseko is not a footnote. It changes what we can do and how quickly we can do it. No travel fees, no coordination delays across time zones, and a firsthand understanding of what makes a Niseko property worth photographing well.

We understand the seasonal conditions, the light at different times of year, the ski access points that matter to international buyers, and the architectural details that distinguish a well-built Hokkaido property from a generic resort unit.

  • No travel costs: we are already here. No surcharges for properties across the Niseko area including Kutchan, Hirafu, Annupuri and Hanazono.
  • Seasonal expertise: we know how to shoot Niseko properties in snow, in summer green and at the in-between shoulder seasons that most photographers avoid.
  • International buyer perspective: having worked extensively in New Zealand and Japan, we understand what buyers from Sydney, Singapore and Hong Kong are actually looking for.
  • JCAB drone compliance: we operate under Japan Civil Aviation Bureau regulations and hold the relevant permits for aerial work in the Niseko area.

About us

Operated by
Kiwi Photography
Godo Kaisha

Property Media Japan is operated by Kiwi Photography Godo Kaisha, a Japanese registered company based in Niseko, Hokkaido. We have been shooting real estate professionally since 2015, starting in Queenstown, New Zealand, where we photographed over 3,000 residential and commercial properties across a decade.

We brought that experience to Niseko because the market needed it. International buyers expect international presentation standards. The photography here should reflect that.

Alongside real estate, we operate Niseko Memories (lifestyle and portrait photography) and are affiliated with Nomad Weddings Japan for destination wedding photography across Hokkaido.
