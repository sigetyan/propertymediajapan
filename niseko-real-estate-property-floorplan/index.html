Why floorplans matter

Buyers need
to understand space

Photos tell buyers what a property looks like. Floorplans tell them how it works. Room layout, flow between spaces, the relationship between indoor and outdoor areas. For buyers comparing properties remotely, a floorplan is often the deciding factor between making an enquiry and moving on.

The Niseko market draws international buyers who are purchasing properties they may not visit in person until after the sale. A floorplan is not a nice-to-have for these buyers. It is a basic requirement.

Available as a standalone order or added to any photography package. Turnaround is 48 hours from the date of measurement.

What we offer

Three formats
for every need

01

2D Floorplan

A measured, to-scale 2D floorplan drafted from on-site measurements. Room dimensions, door and window positions, and total floor area clearly indicated. The standard format for portal listings and marketing brochures.

Delivery: 48 hours  |  Format: JPG, PDF, PNG
02

3D Floorplan

A three-dimensional rendered view of the floorplan showing ceiling heights, furniture placement and spatial relationships between rooms. More intuitive than 2D for buyers unfamiliar with reading architectural drawings.

Delivery: 48 hours  |  Format: JPG, PDF, PNG
03

CGI Rendering

Photorealistic CGI renders for off-plan properties, development projects and architectural presentations. Shows the finished building before construction is complete, with accurate materials, lighting and landscaping.

Delivery: on request  |  Format: JPG, PNG, high-res print

Virtual staging

Empty rooms
furnished digitally

A furnished property sells faster than an empty one. Virtual staging adds photorealistic CGI furniture and decor to vacant rooms, giving buyers a clear sense of how a space can be lived in without the cost and logistics of physical staging. Particularly effective for off-season listings and investment properties where the unit is empty between tenants.

CGI and architectural rendering

Sell it before
it is built

For developers and architects working on new builds or off-plan projects in Niseko, CGI rendering allows you to market a property before construction is complete. Photorealistic exterior and interior renders produced from architectural drawings and specifications.

Used by developers to pre-sell units, by architects for client presentations, and by agents marketing off-plan stock to international buyers.

  • Off-plan sales: show buyers a finished property before the foundation is laid
  • Architect presentations: photorealistic renders for client approvals and planning submissions
  • Development marketing: hero images for brochures, websites and portal listings
  • Interior design: pre-visualise material choices, finishes and furniture layouts before fitting out
