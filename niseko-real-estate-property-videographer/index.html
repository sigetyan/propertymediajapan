Why video

Photography shows.
Video convinces.

Photography captures a moment. Video moves through a space, shows how rooms connect, and gives buyers a genuine sense of scale and flow. For a buyer in Singapore or Sydney comparing Niseko properties they cannot visit in person, a two-minute walkthrough video does the work of ten viewings.

Today's buyers expect video. Properties listed without it are already at a disadvantage on portals and social media, where video content holds attention and drives enquiries at a rate stills alone cannot match.

We work with agents to understand the property and its selling points before we shoot. The result is a video that is structured, paced and edited to do one thing: generate enquiries.

What we produce

Three types of property video

01

Property walkthrough

A smooth, cinematic move through the property from entrance to living areas, bedrooms, bathrooms and outdoor spaces. The standard format for residential listings. Edited to one to two minutes with music and colour grading.

Delivery: 48 hours  |  Format: MP4, 1080p or 4K
02

Aerial and ground combo

Combines interior and exterior ground footage with JCAB-licensed drone aerial shots. Shows the property in its full Niseko context: ski access, mountain views, section size and proximity to the village. The most complete format for premium listings.

Delivery: 48 hours  |  Requires: JCAB clearance if applicable
03

Short-form social cut

A 30 to 60 second vertical cut optimised for Instagram Reels, TikTok and LINE. Produced from the same footage as the main walkthrough at no additional shoot cost. Formatted for mobile-first viewing where most property discovery now happens.

Delivery: 48 hours  |  Format: Vertical 9:16, MP4
How it works

From brief
to delivered

We keep the production process straightforward. No lengthy pre-production meetings or complicated approval chains. You brief us, we shoot, we edit, you receive the finished file.

01

Brief and booking

Fill in the enquiry form with the property address, your preferred shoot date and any specific shots you want included. We confirm availability and pricing within a few hours.

02

Pre-shoot prep

We walk through the property brief with you before the shoot day. Agents are guided through the process and encouraged to share any ideas. We plan the shot sequence to make the most of available time.

03

Shoot day

A standard residential video shoot takes two to three hours. Aerial add-ons are scheduled to coincide with suitable weather and light conditions, in line with JCAB requirements.

04

Edit and delivery

The finished video is delivered via download link within 48 hours. Files are formatted for portal upload, social media and direct sharing. One round of minor revisions is included.
